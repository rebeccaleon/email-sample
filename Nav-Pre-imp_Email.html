We’d like to introduce a part of your benefits that will help make health care less complicated. Soon, Blue Cross Coordinated Care Navigator℠ will be available to you and your eligible family members at no additional cost.

Clinical navigators are registered nurses who will help guide you along your health care journey. It’s like having a nurse in the family to turn to with your medical concerns. They’ll:
  • Guide and support you in making decisions
  • Give you real‑time advice to get the best care available for you or your family members’ specific needs
  • Help you understand medical concerns like diagnoses, treatment plans, test results and more
  • Assist you with scheduling appointments
Starting soon, if you have a medical concern, you’ll be able to call the number on the back of your Blue Cross member ID card to be connected with a clinical navigator. Or, use the BCBSM Coordinated Care℠ app to conveniently stay connected with your clinical navigator.
